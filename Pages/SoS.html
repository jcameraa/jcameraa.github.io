Saving Our Singles

This game was the first time I participated in a Game Jam, and was a great experience! We had about 11 members in our group, and this is the largest group I have worked on while for making games to date.

This game is a dating simulator, so we had to have many people on narrative design, and I was one of them! First, the narrative people collaboratively discussed how we wanted the game to play and what emotions we wanted each character to give. Once we settled on that, we began writing the characters we were assigned, and we would regularly check in with everyone to confirm the story is advancing how we intended it to.

Saving our Singles was my first introduction to digital game design, and I thoroughly enjoyed it. The characters we were able to develop were dynamic, and the world-building we were able to do was something to be proud of.
