Alice ecilA

This is my most current project, and I plan on updating this every 2 weeks with progress!

While designing this game, my team and I drew from multiple sources. The first one we wanted to implement in Alice in Wonderland, as we wanted to create a place to go if you fail at a level, and this place would be “wonderland” This inspiration can be seen in its name and aesthetic. The story we want to tell is one of someone who suffers from depression and feels that they are losing a part of themselves to mental illness. We want our game to remind people that

The goal with this project is to make a 2D platformer that manipulates the way the character interacts with the world through different platforms. This will be the first time I will be implementing art for a game, so I will be updated the website and stating which art is mine! This is a group of only 3 people, so each person has multiple roles, with some overlapping. My main role is programming and design, but I am also helping in narrative design and art.
