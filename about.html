About Me

Hi, I'm Jamie Camera

Thank you for visiting my website! I am a second-year student at Northeastern University pursuing a degree in Computer Science and Game Design with an expected graduation date of May 2021. I love making games, and my goal is to continue my education in them and make spectacular games for others to enjoy. I intend to focus on game programming and game design, and I would love to gain more experience in game production!

I have had various roles in my group experiences with games, ranging from lead programmer to narrative design. I have worked in groups as large as 11 to make a game in 48 hours, as well as concepting, designing, and implementing a game by myself. The variety of projects I have worked on has taught me a vast set of skills, namely game programming and game design, all of which I hope to utilize as I further my career.

My education at Northeastern has greatly taught me what it means to make games, and with every class I take and project I work on I continue to build my skills. Besides creating games in classes, I have also made games recreationally, especially during Game Jams. Since I started my path toward a Game Design degree in September 2017, I have attended two Global Game Jams, and this past year have participated in the Anti-Defamation League Game Jam. All of these experiences have been some of my favorites in regards to game design, and they further ignite my passion for creating meaningful games.

All in all, I truly love creating games and am excited to continue making them. I have the communication skills required to work in a collaborative industry and I display this when I work on teams. I believe in learning something from every experience, no matter how well the final piece turns out to be, and I have been lucky enough to produce games I am proud of and have also learned from! I am very eager to enter the game design and production industry, and I would love to discuss any projects and opportunities. I can be reached through my email at Camera.j@husky.neu.edu
